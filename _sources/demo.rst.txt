Scenarios
---------

**Part 0: Platform Overview**
   Cluster setup, status, available methods, datasets, and experiments.

**Part 1: Comparative Benchmarking**
   Comparing DOMINANT vs CONAD on bond_inj_cora with standardized evaluation.

**Part 2: Custom Method Integration**
   Integrating TADDY with GraFlag using the ``graflag_runner`` framework.

**Part 3: Reproducibility Verification**
   Reproducing an experiment from its saved ``service_config.json``.

**GUI Dashboard Demo**
   Live walkthrough of the web dashboard including running DOMINANT on bond_inj_cora,
   viewing evaluation results, and browsing experiment plots.
