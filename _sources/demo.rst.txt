Scenarios
---------

**GUI Dashboard**
   Live walkthrough of the web dashboard: cluster status, launching experiments,
   streaming logs, and viewing evaluation metrics and plots.

**CLI Overview**
   Cluster setup, status, and browsing available methods and datasets from the terminal.

**Scenario 1: Comparative Benchmarking**
   Comparing DOMINANT vs CONAD on bond_inj_cora with automated evaluation.

**Scenario 2: Custom Method Integration**
   Integrating TADDY into the framework using the ``graflag_runner`` SDK.

**Scenario 3: Reproducibility Verification**
   Reproducing an experiment from its saved ``service_config.json`` and comparing results.
